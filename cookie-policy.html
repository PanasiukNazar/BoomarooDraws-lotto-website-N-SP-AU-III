Cookie Policy

Welcome to our Cookie Policy. Here we explain how we use cookies and similar technologies to collect information when you use our AU Lotto services. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle cookies related to AU Lotto.

I. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you interact with our AU Lotto platform. We use cookies to enhance your user experience and collect useful information such as your preferences and settings. Cookies help us deliver advanced features related specifically to lotto services, such as personalized lotto number suggestions and participation tracking.

II. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are crucial for the proper functioning of our lotto services. Without them, essential features like login authentication and user preference management would not work.

2.2 Performance Cookies

Performance cookies collect data on how users interact with our AU Lotto platform to help us improve service quality. They allow us to monitor lotto game participation and optimize user experience.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies remember your choices, such as language settings and lotto notification preferences, to provide a personalized lotto experience.

2.4 Targeting Cookies

Targeting cookies gather information about your interaction with AU Lotto to deliver relevant lotto promotions and content tailored to your interests.

III. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookie preferences directly through your browser settings. Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can modify settings to reject cookies or receive alerts when cookies are sent to your device. Disabling cookies may limit certain features of AU Lotto services, such as personalized lotto alerts.

IV. Use of Similar Technologies

Besides cookies, we may use tracking pixels and web beacons to gather information on how you use our AU Lotto services. These technologies help us analyze engagement with lotto games and promotions to enhance your experience.

V. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update this Cookie Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how cookies are used in AU Lotto. Continued use of our services after updates means you accept the revised policy.
