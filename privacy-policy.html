Welcome to our Privacy Policy. Here, we explain how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our AU Lotto services. I. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Personal Data

To provide and improve our AU Lotto services, we collect certain types of information.

When you participate in AU Lotto or interact with our platform, we may ask for personal information that can identify or contact you ("personal data"). This may include, but is not limited to:

We also collect data automatically about how you access and use our AU Lotto services ("usage data"). This may include your IP address, browser type and version, pages visited, date and time of access, time spent on pages, and diagnostic data.

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to gather information and enhance your AU Lotto experience. Cookies are small data files with unique identifiers transferred to your device’s storage.
